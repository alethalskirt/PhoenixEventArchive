The chairs that had been set up there earlier were all cleared away. He could see that the Admiral’s grave had been filled in. The flowers now decorated it like snow over the small patch of freshly dug earth. The moon fell over everything, giving a silvery gilding to the headstones. On the other side of the graveyard, the lights of San Francisco glowed brightly with warm yellow and orange tones. There was a soft breeze that stirred Ciel’s hair and tail.
