Mhm, it was going to be difficult to coax her out of here. It was like she had just stepped aboard a cruise ship. And that was what a Starfleet vessel was, at the end of the day – a cruise ship where you could work upon. All of those hints of trepidation seemed to wash away. It felt nice to be back… And even better to be aboard a ship such as this. Silently pacing through the small study area she took note of the message that had been sent by Commander Kunzo. Unfortunately it had been sent the day before so she was likely too late, and as a result she swiftly sent back a note of apology. It was the least she could do. Figuring that she had better be prepared in case any further messages came through – and knowing that she would need to meet department heads and go for her physical – she decided to set about sorting out her quarters for now.
