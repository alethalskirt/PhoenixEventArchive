Kainay 11-Aug-20 07:40 PM
Liano had been here for a little while now. Earlier on she had crept through the hallway with a distinct look of nervousness. Her hands had been held together in front of her, thumbs interlocked and head bowed which created a look which was the height of timidness. To her mass relief things had been perfectly fine so far. The computer had guided her from the airlock to the turbolift just like she had been told it would. Then it had even been kind enough to do the same to take her to her quarters. Every person she had walked past seemed to have noticed her nervousness and for the most part she had been ignored or fixed with a little smile. By the end of her navigation to where she now stayed she had managed to return their smiles along the way. Apparently she had a roommate but they must have been not in right now. It was a shame because Liano was already desperate to know someone aboard the ship and make a friend quickly. She didn't feel the need to rely on them or anything, it was just nice to have someone to speak to when you were entering an unfamiliar environment for the first time! Conscious of the space which might have belonged to her roommate she went around decorating but did not move a single existing item just in case. Synthetic flowers with swirling colours were pinned up along the length of the ceiling like garlands to create a more welcoming atmosphere. Pastel colours seemed to be her thing (everything had to be muted pastel or dull washed out to not contrast with her green skin). This was displayed in a couple pillows here, a blanket over there... Maybe some wall art would be nice!
There was only so much you could do at one time and so after unpacking and putting away clothing and toiletries she then began to think over what to do next. Normally you had to check in for some sort of physical, right? It seemed silly because she had just graduated from the Academy... But it was her first posting and she didn't want to break protocol! The ship was at Earth Spacedock so she might have time to get it done if they were not sent out anywhere so maybe it could wait... But then again she might forget about it later and what if she was needed on duty and hadn't got it done? Best to get it over and done with. The bottom of her uniform was tugged at to get rid of any creases, Starfleet badge straightened, and a stray strand of hair tucked behind one ear. A sudden idea hit her and before she left she activated the computer and left a little note to her roommate. "Hello, my name is Liano and I'm your new roommate! I hope I haven't knocked any of your stuff out of place! Looking forward to meeting you soon!" There we go. That meant if the other person did arrive they wouldn't be confused about all the stuff which was now lying around. Surely it was a good idea! With that sorted she spent another moment in front of the mirror just double checking that she looked okay. Maybe there were other Orions here and she might fit in! A quick mental exercise of psyching herself up with a 'you can do it' was then performed to try to wash away the nervousness. Before she could get more scared and change her mind she left her quarters. Time to get to know the ship and hopefully pass a physical!

Liano had returned to her quarters after going for her physical. She had tried to get in touch with her department head to check in and get ready for duty but she hadn't been able to find the man. Maybe she had showed up at a bad time or got his shift pattern wrong or something. Being a newly graduated ensign meant that she was bound to end up working awkward shifts compared to all of the more experienced officers. It seemed like she was destined for beta or gamma shift but at least it would give her time to settle in without being too busy. Just now she was sitting on her bed cross-legged with the blankets bundled all around her. She had decided to take some time to look over the ship layout, schematics, and crew. Anything she could get her hands on really. There was no way of knowing what sort of administrative or archiving work she might end up doing so she thought she needed to be prepared. Maybe she would be filing away other operational reports or she could end up making sure that engineering records got filed correctly. It would make sense to know a little bit in advance! It was no lie that she didn't really understand all of the stuff on the schematic page. Starfleet Academy had taught her the basics but there was no way that she had remembered all of it. There were too many different units of measurement to remember! So instead of that she took a look through the main crew to see who she should know, going over people like the captain and other department heads. It would be a little bit awkward if she saw one of them in the corridor and didn't stop to salute. It was her first time serving on a ship and Liano wanted to give a good impression. While going through the filing structure and familiarising herself with the way everything was laid out she ended up finding a chatroom for the ship. Was she allowed on it? It seemed open. She could check it out in case, right?
