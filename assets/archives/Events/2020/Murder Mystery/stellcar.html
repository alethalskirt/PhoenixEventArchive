The Lieutenant had finished up what he had to do with the starmap and attempted to shut the projection off. However, with several attempts, the projection did not budge. Confused, the Lieutenant went to investigate. He was quite handy with the consoles around here, as he has been working with them for his entire career. There below the main console, was a panel that looked to be tampered with. The Lieutenant reached down to open the panel up and once he did, he saw that many wires had been re-arranged. As strange as it was, the Lieutenant did not bother with it and stood up to press his combadge.
But, before he could. Another problem caught his eye. An expertly placed distraction that he was unaware about. Instead of the panel flashing bright blue, it was flashing bright red. The console below it was spitting out some unknown text and instead of it being simple readings it was something that nobody wanted to see. Once decrypted the Lieutenant read out the words “In the shadows, they strike. In the light, they strike, you are not safe”. Scared, the Lieutenant, now more than ever, urgently whipped his hand up to his combadge. But before he could touch the badge, 2 hands were so gently placed in front of his mouth and to the side of his ear. In one swift move the Lieutenants neck snapped. His limp body falling to the floor in an instant with a dull thud. His eyes, wide open showing that the last thing he saw, was the work that got him into this mess. As for the culprit, they left without any traces. The expert made an escape as soon as the body had fallen to the floor.
