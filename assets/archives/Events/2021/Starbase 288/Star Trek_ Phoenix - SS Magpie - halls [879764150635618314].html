[D] When the party arrived in the Magpie's hall, the first thing they noticed was how hot it was. The air was wet, and all the bulkheads were slicked over with little rivulets of water. There was even a bit of a mist hanging above the ground. The heavy smell of wet earth and growing things choked out any normal ship smells. All over the walls, and even from the ceiling, rotting pieces of wood were strung. Thousands of small, round fungi crowded over these, packing as many as would fit onto the logs. The fungi gave off a bright orange glow, and actually did a better job of lighting the space than the dim, reddish emergency lighting. The hall was small and somewhat claustrophobic. The ship had clearly been heavily modified with a mishmash of different technologies. The bulkhead which they were standing next to clearly had a half a registry number and name which was not the Magpie's emblazoned on it: the USS Nightinga- (The rest of the name was cut off by the next panel).
