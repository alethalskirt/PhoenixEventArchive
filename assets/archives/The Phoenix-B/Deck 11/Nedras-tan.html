The second was space. Most Dominion ships, or at least the one he'd lived on for half his life, tended to sacrifice comfort for efficiency. He unpacked first, setting up the various trinkets he'd been told were a necessity by his Academy roommate to 'show personality' - poster of a randomly selected Earth band, unusually shaped antique-style lamp, sculpture of a stylized cat he'd picked up at Earth Spacedock on his way in - before sitting down on his bed and seeing what information the PADD offered. The physical would come next, of course, but it was unwise to go into enemy territory without briefing himself first. Wait - no. New, friendly territory. Without learning a little more about it first. Yes. He checked the floorplan first, then saw there was apparently a chatroom. There was a good first order of business. Past public communication.
