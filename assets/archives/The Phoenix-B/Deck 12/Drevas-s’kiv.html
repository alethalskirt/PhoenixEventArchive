Vedek defrocked for extremist views? His brow furrowed as he pulled the article up and read it over. It wasn't often that someone of such a high position was knocked off the proverbial throne...
Late Friday night, well-known Vedek Drevas Aarco was declared stripped of his position by unanimous vote from the Assembly. He was found to have held extreme prejudice towards Bajorans who do not choose to place their faith in the Prophets, decrying them as false members of our people and heretics who did not deserve the...
He didn't know what to think or feel. Maybe it was the fact that the local press had put his father's smiling face in the article: serene, amiable, hair neatly kept as it had always been. That was not the face of an extremist and he knew that. Or maybe it was the realization of how wrong things could possibly go. This was one such way. Either way he couldn't bear to read the rest - he shut the PADD off and set it aside, holding his head in his hands. Why. Why of all the vedeks to lose their place on the Assembly, did it have to be his father?
